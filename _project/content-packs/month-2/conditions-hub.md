## Page Content

### Introduction

Dr. Imran Qureshi is a board-certified interventional spine and sports medicine physician specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of chronic pain conditions. Whether you are dealing with persistent back pain, nerve pain that radiates down your leg, or a sports injury that is not healing, our Katy, TX office offers advanced, minimally invasive treatment options tailored to your specific condition.

Below are the conditions most commonly treated at our practice. If you do not see your condition listed, call us — Dr. Qureshi treats a wide range of musculoskeletal and neurological pain conditions.

### Spine Conditions

**Low Back Pain**
The most common reason patients visit our office. Low back pain can stem from disc herniations, facet joint arthritis, muscle strain, spinal stenosis, or degenerative disc disease. Dr. Qureshi uses a combination of physical examination and advanced imaging to identify the specific source of your pain and develop a targeted treatment plan.
→ [Learn more about low back pain treatment](/low-back-pain)

**Herniated & Bulging Discs**
A herniated disc occurs when the soft inner material of a spinal disc pushes through the outer layer, potentially compressing nearby nerve roots. Symptoms include radiating pain, numbness, tingling, and weakness in the legs or arms depending on the location. The majority of herniated discs respond to nonsurgical treatment including epidural steroid injections and physical therapy.
→ [Learn more about herniated disc treatment](/bulging/herniated-discs)

**Neck Pain & Arm Numbness**
Cervical spine conditions — including disc herniations, cervical radiculopathy, and facet joint arthritis — can cause persistent neck pain along with numbness, tingling, or weakness that radiates into the shoulder, arm, and hand. Treatment options include cervical epidural injections, nerve blocks, and radiofrequency ablation.
→ [Learn more about neck pain treatment](/neck-pain-and-arm-numbness)

**Sacroiliac (SI) Joint Pain**
The sacroiliac joint connects your spine to your pelvis and is a commonly overlooked source of lower back and buttock pain. SI joint dysfunction can mimic sciatica or lumbar disc problems. Accurate diagnosis through SI joint injections, followed by targeted treatment, can provide significant relief.
→ [Learn more about SI joint pain treatment](/sacroiliac-si-joint-pain)

### Nerve Conditions

**Neuropathy & Nerve Pain**
Peripheral neuropathy and nerve-related pain conditions cause burning, tingling, numbness, and sharp shooting pain — most commonly in the hands and feet. Causes include diabetes, injury, compression, and idiopathic (unknown) origins. Dr. Qureshi offers nerve blocks, spinal cord stimulation, and other advanced treatments for nerve pain that has not responded to medication alone.
→ [Learn more about neuropathy treatment](/nerve-pain/neuropathy)

**Sciatica**
Sciatica describes radiating pain from the lower back through the buttock and down the leg, following the path of the sciatic nerve. It is typically caused by a herniated disc or spinal stenosis compressing a nerve root. Epidural steroid injections are one of the most effective nonsurgical treatments for sciatica, often providing relief within days.
→ [Learn more about sciatica and nerve pain treatment](/nerve-pain/neuropathy)

### Joint Conditions

**Knee Pain & Arthritis**
Knee osteoarthritis, meniscal tears, and chronic knee pain can significantly limit mobility and quality of life. Treatment options at our practice include joint injections, PRP therapy, and nerve blocks targeting the genicular nerves of the knee.
→ [Learn more about knee pain treatment](/knee-pain/arthritis)

**Hip Pain & Arthritis**
Hip joint arthritis, bursitis, and labral pathology cause pain in the groin, lateral hip, and buttock that can make walking, climbing stairs, and sleeping difficult. Dr. Qureshi offers hip joint injections, PRP therapy, and targeted nerve treatments for hip-related pain.
→ [Learn more about hip pain treatment](/hip-pain/arthritis)

**Shoulder Pain & Injuries**
Rotator cuff injuries, frozen shoulder, shoulder arthritis, and impingement syndrome respond well to image-guided shoulder injections and regenerative treatments. Whether your shoulder pain is from an acute injury or chronic wear and tear, we can help.
→ [Learn more about shoulder pain treatment](/shoulder-injuries/pain)

### Sports & Activity-Related Conditions

**Sports Injuries**
From weekend warriors to competitive athletes, sports injuries — including sprains, strains, tendinitis, and overuse injuries — can sideline you from the activities you love. Dr. Qureshi's dual board certification in interventional spine and sports medicine provides a comprehensive approach to getting you back to your sport safely.
→ [Learn more about sports injury treatment](/sports-injuries)
