Imran Qureshi, D.O.

Interventional Spine & Sports Medicine Physician ¬∑ Katy, TX

Dr. Imran Qureshi is a board-certified interventional spine and sports medicine physician serving patients in Katy, Cinco Ranch, Fulshear, and the Greater Houston area. He holds a Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine (D.O.) degree and completed advanced fellowship training in interventional spine and musculoskeletal medicine, equipping him with the specialized skills to diagnose and treat complex spinal and joint conditions.

Dr. Qureshi founded his practice on a simple but powerful philosophy: every patient deserves a precise diagnosis and a targeted treatment plan ‚Äî one that avoids unnecessary surgery, reduces dependence on opioids, and restores real quality of life. He approaches pain management with a whole-person perspective that is central to osteopathic training, looking beyond the immediate symptom to understand how the entire musculoskeletal system is involved.

With over a decade of clinical experience, Dr. Qureshi performs a wide range of image-guided, minimally invasive procedures including epidural steroid injections, radiofrequency ablation, spinal cord stimulation, PRP therapy, nerve blocks, and joint injections. He performs procedures under fluoroscopic and ultrasound guidance to ensure pinpoint accuracy and optimal outcomes.

Dr. Qureshi takes time with each patient ‚Äî explaining findings clearly, walking through all available options, and building a long-term treatment partnership. His 5-star patient reviews consistently highlight his thoroughness, compassion, and the genuine relief his patients experience.

Care Philosophy

Non-surgical relief, targeted treatment, real results

Too many patients are told surgery is their only option ‚Äî or are handed a prescription and sent home. Dr. Qureshi believes there is almost always a better path. His approach begins with a precise, imaging-guided diagnosis. Once the true source of pain is identified, he develops a personalized, minimally invasive treatment plan designed to deliver lasting relief without the risks, recovery time, or costs of surgery.

As a D.O., Dr. Qureshi sees the whole person ‚Äî not just the MRI. He integrates osteopathic principles with the most current interventional techniques to address both the structural and functional contributors to pain.

01

Precise Diagnosis First

Effective treatment begins with knowing exactly what's causing your pain. Dr. Qureshi uses detailed examination and imaging review to identify the true pain generator before recommending any procedure.

02

Minimally Invasive by Default

Every treatment plan starts conservatively and escalates only when needed. The goal is the least invasive, most targeted intervention that delivers meaningful, durable relief.

Pain management is rarely a one-visit fix. Dr. Qureshi works with patients over time ‚Äî adjusting plans, monitoring progress, and always keeping the patient's long-term function and quality of life at the center.
