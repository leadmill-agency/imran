Conditions We Treat · Katy, TX

Conditions We Treat

Dr. Imran Qureshi is a board-certified interventional spine and sports medicine physician specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of chronic pain conditions. Whether you are dealing with persistent back pain, nerve pain that radiates down your leg, or a sports injury that is not healing, our Katy, TX office offers advanced, minimally invasive treatment options tailored to your specific condition.

Below are the conditions most commonly treated at our practice. If you do not see your condition listed, call us — Dr. Qureshi treats a wide range of musculoskeletal and neurological pain conditions.

Spine Conditions

Low Back Pain

The most common reason patients visit our office. Low back pain can stem from disc herniations, facet joint arthritis, muscle strain, spinal stenosis, or degenerative disc disease.

Learn more ›

Herniated & Bulging Discs

A herniated disc occurs when the soft inner material of a spinal disc pushes through the outer layer, potentially compressing nearby nerve roots and causing radiating pain, numbness, and weakness.

Learn more ›

Neck Pain & Arm Numbness

Cervical spine conditions — including disc herniations, cervical radiculopathy, and facet joint arthritis — can cause persistent neck pain along with numbness, tingling, or weakness radiating into the arm.

Learn more ›

Sacroiliac (SI) Joint Pain

The sacroiliac joint connects your spine to your pelvis and is a commonly overlooked source of lower back and buttock pain. SI joint dysfunction can mimic sciatica or lumbar disc problems.

Learn more ›

Nerve Conditions

Neuropathy & Nerve Pain

Peripheral neuropathy and nerve-related pain conditions cause burning, tingling, numbness, and sharp shooting pain — most commonly in the hands and feet. Causes include diabetes, injury, and compression.

Learn more ›

Sciatica

Sciatica describes radiating pain from the lower back through the buttock and down the leg, following the path of the sciatic nerve. It is typically caused by a herniated disc or spinal stenosis.

Learn more ›

Joint Conditions

Knee Pain & Arthritis

Knee osteoarthritis, meniscal tears, and chronic knee pain can significantly limit mobility and quality of life. Treatment options include joint injections, PRP therapy, and genicular nerve blocks.

Learn more ›

Hip Pain & Arthritis

Hip joint arthritis, bursitis, and labral pathology cause pain in the groin, lateral hip, and buttock that can make walking, climbing stairs, and sleeping difficult.

Learn more ›

Shoulder Pain & Injuries

Rotator cuff injuries, frozen shoulder, shoulder arthritis, and impingement syndrome respond well to image-guided shoulder injections and regenerative treatments.

Learn more ›

Sports & Activity-Related

Sports Injuries

From weekend warriors to competitive athletes, sports injuries — including sprains, strains, tendinitis, and overuse injuries — can sideline you from the activities you love. Dr. Qureshi's dual board certification provides a comprehensive approach to recovery.

Learn more ›

Other Conditions We Treat

Beyond the conditions above, Dr. Qureshi regularly treats:

  • Spinal stenosis
  • Degenerative disc disease
  • Facet joint syndrome
  • Post-surgical pain (failed back surgery syndrome)
  • Complex regional pain syndrome (CRPS)
  • Vertebral compression fractures
  • Chronic headaches (cervicogenic)
  • Myofascial pain syndrome
  • Fibromyalgia-related pain
  • Work-related injuries
  • Auto accident injuries
