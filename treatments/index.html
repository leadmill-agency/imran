Our Treatment Options · Katy, TX

Pain Management Treatments in Katy, TX

Dr. Imran Qureshi offers a full range of minimally invasive, interventional pain management treatments at his Katy, TX office. Every treatment plan begins with an accurate diagnosis, followed by a personalized approach that targets the source of your pain directly.

Our goal is to reduce your pain, restore your function, and help you avoid surgery whenever possible. Most procedures are performed in-office under fluoroscopic (live X-ray) guidance, take less than an hour, and allow you to return home the same day.

Interventional Procedures

Epidural Steroid Injections

Targeted anti-inflammatory medication delivered to inflamed nerve roots in the spine. One of the most effective nonsurgical treatments for herniated discs, spinal stenosis, sciatica, and radiculopathy.

Learn more ›

Radiofrequency Ablation

Uses targeted heat energy to disrupt pain signals from specific nerves, providing relief that can last 6 months to 2 years. Especially effective for facet joint pain, sacroiliac joint pain, and chronic neck or back pain.

Learn more ›

Nerve Blocks

Targeted anesthetic and anti-inflammatory injections to specific nerves, serving both diagnostic and therapeutic purposes. Types include medial branch blocks, selective nerve root blocks, occipital nerve blocks, and more.

Learn more ›

Joint Injections

Anti-inflammatory injections into facet joints, sacroiliac joints, and peripheral joints (knee, hip, shoulder) to reduce pain from arthritis, injury, or inflammation. Performed under fluoroscopic or ultrasound guidance.

Learn more ›

Trigger Point Injections

Targeted injections into painful muscle knots (myofascial trigger points) to relieve chronic muscle pain, tension headaches, and myofascial pain syndrome. Quick in-office procedure with minimal downtime.

Learn more ›

Spinal Cord Stimulation

An implantable device that sends mild electrical pulses to the spinal cord, interrupting pain signals before they reach the brain. For patients with chronic pain that has not responded to other treatments.

Learn more ›

Regenerative Medicine

PRP Therapy (Platelet-Rich Plasma)

Uses concentrated platelets from your own blood to promote tissue repair and reduce inflammation. Treats chronic joint pain, tendon injuries, mild to moderate osteoarthritis, and degenerative disc conditions.

Learn more ›

Diagnostic Procedures

Discography

A diagnostic procedure that identifies which intervertebral disc is causing your chronic back pain. By pressurizing each disc and evaluating your pain response, discography provides precision that MRI alone cannot.

Learn more ›

Diagnostic Imaging

Comprehensive imaging services including X-ray and fluoroscopy to support accurate diagnosis and guide interventional procedures. Advanced imaging is the foundation of precise, safe treatment.

Learn more ›

Spine Procedures

Kyphoplasty

A minimally invasive procedure to treat vertebral compression fractures caused by osteoporosis, trauma, or cancer. A balloon restores vertebral height, and bone cement stabilizes the fracture. Most patients experience significant pain relief within 48 hours.

Learn more ›

Our Approach

Dr. Qureshi believes in exhausting nonsurgical options before considering surgery. Every patient receives a thorough evaluation, a clear explanation of their diagnosis, and a treatment plan designed around their specific pain pattern, goals, and lifestyle. Most procedures are covered by major insurance plans.
