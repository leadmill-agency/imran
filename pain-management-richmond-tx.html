Interventional Pain Care for Richmond Patients

Residents of Richmond no longer have to travel far into Houston for access to specialist-level interventional pain management. Dr. Imran Qureshi’s practice is conveniently located at 23501 Cinco Ranch Blvd, Suite G205 in Katy — just 15 minutes from downtown Richmond and easily accessible via Highway 99 / Grand Parkway.

Dr. Qureshi specializes in minimally invasive, image-guided procedures that address the root cause of chronic pain rather than simply masking symptoms. His approach emphasizes precise diagnosis, targeted treatment, and measurable improvement in daily function and quality of life.

Conditions Treated

Dr. Qureshi treats a wide range of acute and chronic musculoskeletal and spine conditions for patients throughout Fort Bend County and Greater Houston:

  • Chronic low back pain and lumbar disc herniation
  • Neck pain, cervical disc disease, and arm numbness / radiculopathy
  • Sciatica and nerve pain (neuropathy)
  • Facet joint arthritis and sacroiliac (SI) joint dysfunction
  • Knee arthritis and hip pain
  • Shoulder injuries, rotator cuff pain, bursitis
  • Sports injuries and musculoskeletal overuse conditions
  • Post-surgical pain and failed back surgery syndrome

Treatment Options

Using image-guided (fluoroscopy and ultrasound) techniques, Dr. Qureshi offers the full range of interventional pain procedures:

Epidural Steroid Injections

Targeted relief for herniated discs, sciatica, and spinal stenosis

Radiofrequency Ablation

Long-lasting facet joint pain relief — often 12–18 months

Nerve Blocks

Diagnostic and therapeutic blocks for targeted pain relief

PRP Therapy

Regenerative treatment using your own platelet-rich plasma

Joint Injections

Corticosteroid and viscosupplement injections for knee, hip, shoulder

Spinal Cord Stimulation

Advanced neuromodulation for chronic, refractory pain conditions
